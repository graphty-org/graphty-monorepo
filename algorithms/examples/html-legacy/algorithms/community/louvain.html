Louvain Community Detection Explained

Louvain finds groups of tightly connected nodes, like finding friend groups in a social network.

It optimizes modularity - a measure of how well a network divides into communities.

Key Concept:

Louvain community detection works by:

  • Modularity optimization: Measures quality of community division
  • Two-phase approach: Local optimization followed by aggregation
  • Resolution parameter: Controls community size (higher = smaller communities)

Communities represent groups of nodes that are more densely connected to each other than to the rest of the network.
